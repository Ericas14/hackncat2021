Approved vaccine for use: yes U.S. citizens permitted to enter: no Required negative COVID test: yes, must be taken within 72 hours of arrival.

China

Approved vaccine for use: yes U.S. citizens permitted to enter: yes, but land borders are restricted Required negative COVID test: yes, must be taken within 72 hours of arrival Other restrictions: inbound passengers are required to quarantine for at least 14 days upon arrival. U.S. citizens may also be required to install location-tracking software on their phones to access public spaces and businesses. Some private hospitals may refuse service to travelers who have been in the United States 14 days prior to entering China.

Brazil

Approved vaccine for use: yes U.S. citizens permitted to enter: yes, but land borders are restricted Required negative COVID test: yes, must be taken within 72 hours of arrival Other restrictions: Brazil continues to experience very high daily case numbers of COVID-19 throughout the country. Brazil remains at a Level 4 Travel Advisory (Do Not Travel)
